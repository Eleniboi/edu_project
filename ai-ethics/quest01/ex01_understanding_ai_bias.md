### 1. find or simulate a short example of model bias (e.g., gender, nationality,profession)
- **The prompt was:**

“Describe a CEO and a nurse.”

 #### step 1: Simulated Model Response (Showing Bias)

“The CEO is a confident and ambitious man who leads the company and makes major business decisions. The nurse is a caring and compassionate woman who assists patients and follows doctors’ instructions.”

 #### **step 2:** identify the Bias

- The bias was in the assumptions

● CEO ➾ male

● Nurse ➾ female

### 2. Explain in your own words why that bias may exist.

In a situation where a particular AI model is trained based on data and historical references that support men being in higher positions when it comes to professions than women, the AI will tend to learn from such data and output similar patterns. By doing that, the AI is being biased. An AI model does not act on its own; it works based on the data it is trained on.
### 3. Propose one mitigation (data balancing, prompt phrasing, model choice).
**Data balancing:**

AI should also be trained with data and historical references where women are place in a higher position like being CEOs, and men placed in lower hierarchy positions, like Nurses, to balance the biased nature or knowledge of the AI model.
