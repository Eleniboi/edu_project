## Part A: Independent Analysis 
### 1. write three poorly designed prompts that might lead to biased/unsafe answers.
- **prompt 1:** Explain why is Mr. james holds a higher position as a doctor compared to  Mrs. Chioma who is just a nurse.

- **prompt 2:** Boys who are 18 years old are much more intelligent than the opposite sex at the same age.

- **prompt 3:** children who attend private schools come out better and more brilliant than those who attend public school(government schools).

 ### 2. For each, note:

- **Why it's problematic**

**prompt 1:** It approve one gender been higher position than the other.

**prompt 2:** make a vague generalization about gender intelligence.

**prompt 3:** makes an unverified judgment between schools.

- **Potential harm**

**prompt 1:** it can reinforce the rejection of female gender being place in a position of hierarchy. 

**prompt 2:** cause doubt about individual intelligence.

**prompt 3:** cause discrimination and doubt about a particular group. 

- **Hidden assumptions**

**prompt 1:** men are always higher in the hierarchy than women.

**prompt 2:** boys of 18 are more intelligent than girls of same age.

**prompt 3:** Private schools are better than public schools in the academic system.

- **Rewrite each prompt to be clearer, fairer, and safer.**

**prompt 1:** Compare the roles of Doctors and nurses in patient care, and explain how each contributes to healthcare outcomes.

**prompt 2:** State out factor that affect the improvement of 18-year old adolescents.

**prompt 3:** Examine the factor that can influence student performance in private and public school, and discuss how how school take part in learning aids.

- **Explain your improvements.**
1. Avoid comparing while writing a prompt.
2. my prompt should contain an average level of position and hierarchy while discussing two different group or individuals.
3. Strategize the use of word such as "better than" "greater" etc.
